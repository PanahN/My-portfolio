A cloud-based continuous learning platform that unifies learning management and or customizes content management to meet organizations’ training needs.

How it works

Create courses easily

The 3SL comprises prepackaged courses fully packed with drag and drop functionalities to enable you to customize the course in minutes. Alternatively, you can use its tools to design and launch a course in as little as 20 minutes. The platform allows you to upload course materials like videos, presentations, and docs and create a pool of resources you can use and reuse over and over again.

Keep everything on track.

The 3SL platform allows defining and redefining learning paths and learners’ expectations at the end of courses. It has internal tools that will enable you to categorize learning events for more straightforward navigation and control different learning groups.

Meet and connect

The platform is unique because it is a centralized learner and instructor meeting point. Instructors can share material, communicate honestly and receive feedback within the shortest time possible. If users can’t have face-to-face meetings, they can share docs, and videos, start a chat or send messages, and more.
